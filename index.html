Lessons learned

Lessons learned & best practices

What worked, and what we would refine.

What worked well

  • Start with high-value, repeatable QA tasks.
  • Invest early in prompt and agent libraries.
  • Pair AI with QA engineers — augment, don't replace.
  • Bake in guardrails and human review from day one.
  • Track impact with shared quality metrics.

Watch-outs & refinements

  • Optimize for token efficiency to keep scaling and reduce dependency on evolving pricing models.
  • Manage model & output drift with continuous evals.
  • Build continuous feedback loops to improve agent effectiveness, accuracy and reliability.
  • Treat data privacy and access controls as non-negotiable.
  • Validate AI-generated tests — verify, don't blindly trust.
